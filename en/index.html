About

The Fort d'Arches (Arches' Fort), located in Pouxeux in the Vosges, is a military fortification built in the 19th century as part of the regional defense system. This fort, which is part of the Maginot Line, was designed to withstand enemy attacks and protect the surrounding valleys. It has withstood the test of time, marked by periods of abandonment and repurposing, before becoming an iconic site for history and nature enthusiasts. Today, it has been transformed into a hub for beekeeping, where bees are raised, and ecological initiatives are implemented to preserve local biodiversity.
